Are You OK University Student ?

In recent years, the mental health of university students has become a growing concern. The demands and challenges associated with higher education often take a toll on students' well-being. Numerous factors intertwine with students' mental health, presenting a multifaceted landscape.

Our visualizations aspire to provide users with valuable insights into prevalent stressors, coping mechanisms, and dynamic trends in mental well-being. Armed with this comprehensive understanding, users can collaboratively devise targeted interventions, create proactive support initiatives, and cultivate an environment conducive to enhanced academic success.
